4 Daily Star journalists assaulted

Journos from other outlets also attacked

group of youths, allegedly from the Bangladesh Chhatra League, beats up a student who fell down on the ground in the capital's Jhigatola area during yesterday's clash. Photo: Amran Hossain

Police and alleged BCL men beat up three journalists of The Daily Star and molested another in the capital's Jhigatola yesterday afternoon. One of them, Shaer Reaz, was taking pictures of a clash near Dhanmondi 3/A around 6:00pm when a man, who did not appear to be a law enforcer, started beating him up with a stick, kicked him and punched him and handed him to police.

Biman's special flight cancelled, several hundred Saudi-bound workers protest at Dhaka airport

Star Digital Report
Image

Photo: Star

Several hundred Saudi Arabia bound migrant workers demonstrated at Dhaka Airport early this morning due to cancellation of a Biman Bangladesh Airlines special flight. BG 5039 was scheduled to depart from Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport at 6:15am for Riyadh. But Biman was forced to cancel the special flight as Saudi authorities didn't give landing permission, sources at the HSIA said.
