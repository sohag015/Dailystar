Adieu, legend of silver screen’

In the annals of Bangla cinema she was at one time "Champa", the lone sister of seven brothers, and then in another avatar she was "Kiran Mala", entering the dreams of a generation of film lovers. That "mishti meye" (sweet girl) of Bangla cinema, legendary actor Sarah Begum Kabori who traversed myriad peaks and troughs in her 70 years of life, finally succumbed to Covid-19 early today...
